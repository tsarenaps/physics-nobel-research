## 1903 Nobel Prize in Physics Laureates

- **Antoine Henri Becquerel** (France) – Awarded half of the prize for his discovery of spontaneous radioactivity.
- **Pierre Curie** (France) – Co-recipient of the other half for joint research on radiation phenomena discovered by Becquerel.
- **Marie Skłodowska‑Curie** (Poland/France) – Co-recipient of the other half for joint research on radiation phenomena discovered by Becquerel.

### Sources
- Wikipedia entry for "1903 Nobel Prize in Physics":
  > The Nobel Prize in Physics 1903 was awarded to Antoine Henri Becquerel, Pierre Curie, and Marie Skłodowska‑Curie for their joint researches on the radiation phenomena discovered by Henri Becquerel.
- Nobel Prize official summary (https://www.nobelprize.org/prizes/physics/1903/summary/):
  > The Nobel Prize in Physics 1903 was divided, one half awarded to Antoine Henri Becquerel "in recognition of the extraordinary services he has rendered by his discovery of spontaneous radioactivity", the other half jointly to Pierre Curie and Marie Curie "in recognition of the extraordinary services they have rendered by their joint researches on the radiation phenomena discovered by Professor Henri Becquerel".
